Bibimbap

Description

Bibimbap is a Korean mixed rice dish characterized by a colorful array of vegetables, sliced meat (often beef), and a spicy gochujang (red chili paste) sauce.

Ingredients

  • Cooked short-grain rice
  • Thinly sliced beef
  • Fried egg for topping
  • Gochujang sauce (made with gochujang, soy sauce, sesame oil, and sugar)
  • Sesame oil for drizzling
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Steps

  1. Arrange the cooked rice in a bowl and top it with the sliced beef and a variety of fresh, saut√©ed, or blanched vegetables.
  2. Place a fried egg on top. Drizzle the gochujang sauce and sesame oil over the ingredients.
  3. Garnish with sesame seeds and mix everything together before enjoying your flavorful bibimbap!
